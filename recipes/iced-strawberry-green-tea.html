Iced Strawberry Green Tea

A fruity refreshing drink, perfect for an afternoon tea time. Enjoy with biscuits and good company.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bags Green tea
  • 2 tbsp Strawberry syrup
  • 3/4 cup Ice
  • 1/2 cup Hot water

Instructions:

  1. Heat water to steep green tea according to package instructions. Note that brewing green tea in too high water temperature causes the tea to be bitter.
  2. Fill glass with ice.
  3. After green tea steeps, add strawberry syrup to warm tea and pour over ice.
  4. Enjoy!
