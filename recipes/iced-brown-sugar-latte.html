Iced Brown Sugar Tea Latte

For sugar lovers, this drink tastes like heaven. Add some pearls and you got yourself a homemade bubble tea.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Water
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 bag Black tea
  • 1/2 cup Ice

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and steep black tea according to package directions. Take out tea bag to avoid oversteeping and set aside to cool.
  2. In a pan, mix brown sugar and water. Heat until syrup forms.
  3. Line a glass wall with the brown sugar syrup and add ice.
  4. Add milk into glass to create bottom layer, then add cooled tea. Tip: pour tea over the ice to help separate layers.
  5. Enjoy!
