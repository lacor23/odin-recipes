Lemon Iced Tea

Sweet and tangy, this drink is the perfect refreshment for a hot summer day. Made with black tea, it's an afternoon picker-upper.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bags Black tea
  • 2 tbsp Sweetener
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice or juice from half a lemon
  • 1/2 cup Hot water
  • 3/4 cup Ice

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and steep black tea according to package instructions. Remove tea bags to avoid oversteeping and set aside to cool.
  2. Mix tea with lemon juice and sweeter. Mix thoroughly
  3. Fill glass with ice. Add thinly sliced lemon to sides of the glass. Add tea mixture over ice.
  4. Enjoy!
